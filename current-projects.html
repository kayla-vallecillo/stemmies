water scarcity

This interactive kit tackles the worldwide issue of water scarcity. Water scarcity means that communities face a deficit of fresh, clean water. In this kit, stemmies will create a natural water filter and design modern water solutions to apply in urban settings.

what's in the kit?

This kit includes an interactive zine, which details the water crisis, heroes and change-making organizations, brainstorming guides, coloring pages, and a guide to designing solutions with urban constraints. The zine brings innovators through the important process of exploration, play, design, building, testing, and sharing. The kit also includes materials and instructions to create your own natural water filter using recycled materials.
